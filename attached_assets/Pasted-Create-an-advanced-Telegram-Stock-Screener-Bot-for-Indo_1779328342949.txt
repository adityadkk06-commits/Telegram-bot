SCREENER SYSTEM
========================================

1. ARA HUNTER
Rules:
- Price > MA5
- Price > 1.05 x Previous Price
- Price > Open Price
- Volume > 0.2 x Previous Volume
- Value > 5 Billion IDR

2. BSJP
Rules:
- Value > 10 Billion
- Volume > 1.2 x Previous Volume
- Price > MA20
- MA20 > MA50
- Price > 1.01 x Previous Price
- Price >= MA5
- Volume > 2 x Volume MA20
- Net Foreign Buy Streak >= 2 days

3. BIG ACCUMULATION
Rules:
- Bandar Accumulation/Distribution > 25
- Value > 3 Billion
- MA20 > MA50
- Price < 500
- Volume MA5 > 1.3 x Volume MA20
- Price > Previous Price
